Portfolio

From fully fledged applications to projects I worked on when I was younger, this is a collection of all of my work, findable online and soon to be found in the iOS App Store.

Swift Student Challenge

For 2024, I wanted to get my Swift & SwiftUI up to the next level. And so, I came up with Arithmetic, an app that gamifies learning and makes maths more exciting for young people and children.

This app included daily challanges, various difficulties, and had accessibility features from iOS VoiceOver baked in, preventing visually imparied children from being left behind.

Even better, I am proud to say I was selected as a winner!

Twist Flooring Limited

I was tasked with creating a whole new website for Twist Flooring, a small business in the south of England. Their old site was lagging behind, made from a template and lacking updates. I pushed their new site forward, into the current decade with an all-new modern makeover.

Creating the site, I worked with the owner of Twist Flooring to bring a fresh new feel inspired by their new logo. The site is interactive, and provides more information than the previous one in a well organised manner.

Cooper Accountancy Bath

Cooper Accountancy Bath is a small business based in Bath, United Kingdom - at the time, it was a brand new emerging business, and today it stands proud as a provider for many other businesses in and around Bath.

Working with the owner, I used what I knew to pack in information in a minimalistic and refreshing manner in a way which encompassed the style of their logo and professional nature of the brand.

Coffee+

A Minecraft mod that adds coffee to Minecraft, the right way. Originally made when I was 14, it hasn't seen many updates recently.

Soon, I plan to remake the mod based on the Fabric modloader.
